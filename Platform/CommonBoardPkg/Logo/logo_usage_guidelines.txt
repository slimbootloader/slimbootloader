Slim Bootloader Logo 
To build equity around the project, the Slim Bootloader logo was created for both Intel and community usage. The logo may only be used to represent Slim Bootloader.

Usage Guidelines
The Slim Bootloader logo must be used in connection with truthful, non-misleading references to Slim Bootloader, and for no other purpose. Modification of the logo or use of any separate element(s) of the logo alone are not allowed.
